Why Choose Us

Red White & Clean is a veteran owned pressure washing company proudly serving Kansas City and surrounding communities. Our mission is simple: deliver professional exterior cleaning with the same discipline, reliability, and attention to detail that comes from military service. We specialize in restoring curb appeal for homes and businesses by safely cleaning driveways, siding, roofs, patios, fences, and commercial buildings. Using professional equipment and proven cleaning methods, we remove dirt, algae, mold, and buildup to make your property look fresh and well maintained. At Red White & Clean, we believe in clear communication, honest work, and results that customers can see immediately. Whether it is a driveway that needs to look brand new again or a full house wash to improve the appearance of your property, we treat every job with pride and professionalism. Proudly serving Kansas City and surrounding areas within about an hour drive, we are committed to helping our community keep their homes and businesses looking their best.
